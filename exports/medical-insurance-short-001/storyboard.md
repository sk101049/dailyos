# Storyboard: 有醫療險就夠了嗎？

## Format

- Duration: 30 seconds
- Target format: 9:16 vertical short
- Language: Traditional Chinese
- Tone: calm, educational, trustworthy
- Visual style: clean animated text cards with large mobile-safe subtitles

## Scenes

| Time | Voiceover | Visual | On-screen text |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0-5s | 有醫療險，就真的夠了嗎？ | Opening title with a clean insurance card and hospital signal. | 有醫療險就夠了嗎？ |
| 5-10s | 醫療險主要處理住院、手術和部分醫療支出。 | Three cards slide in for hospital, surgery, medical bills. | 醫療費先被接住 |
| 10-16s | 但療養期間不能工作，收入中斷才可能是壓力。 | Wallet, calendar, and family expense icons appear. | 收入中斷怎麼辦？ |
| 16-22s | 還有看護費、房貸、家庭開銷，不一定都在醫療險裡。 | Comparison card: medical bills vs. living expenses. | 缺口不只醫療費 |
| 22-27s | 保障不是看有沒有，而是看缺口有沒有被補上。 | Checklist highlights medical cost, income replacement, family responsibility. | 檢查三個缺口 |
| 27-30s | 先從保單健檢開始，讓保障跟生活對齊。 | Closing card with calm CTA and DailyOS prototype tag. | 先做保單健檢 |

## Notes

- Educational prototype only; no claim guarantee or product recommendation.
- Keep text short enough for mobile subtitles.
- OpenMontage Explainer currently renders 16:9, then FFmpeg converts the final prototype to 9:16 vertical.
