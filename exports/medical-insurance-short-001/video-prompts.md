# Video Prompt Notes

## Prototype Prompt

Create a clean 30-second vertical insurance education short in Traditional Chinese.
Topic: "有醫療險就夠了嗎？"
Explain that medical insurance helps with hospital, surgery, and medical expenses, but may not cover income interruption, caregiving cost, mortgage pressure, or family living expenses.
Use calm professional visuals, large mobile subtitles, and a trustworthy financial education tone.

## Visual Direction

- Bright white and blue insurance workspace.
- Simple animated cards for medical cost, income replacement, and family responsibility.
- Avoid sales-heavy imagery.
- Avoid claim guarantees.
- End with a soft call to start a policy checkup.
